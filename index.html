Direct Farmer Relationships

Flotsam works directly with farmers who take pride in creating high quality coffee. These close relationships allow us to help support these farmers while also making sure we get fresh green coffee beans to make rich, flavorful coffee.

  • Meet the Farmers

Small Batch Roasting

All of Flotsam's coffee is roasted in small batches, which allows more precision in the roasting process. Flotsam sample's each shipment and pick the best roast for every bean to bring out the flavors we enjoy most about each coffee.

  • See Our Coffees

Crafted Right

The team at Flotsam is trained to not only make a good cup of coffee, but also to know about the entire coffee process and to be knowledgeable about coffee. If you have any questions we're ready to guide your coffee experience.

  • Meet Our Team

Make Yourself At Home

Flotsam aims to be a space where all are welcome. Coffee and community go hand and hand. Whether you need fuel for the day, or a place to spend your time intentionally, Flotsam is here for you.
